Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. General Information

This Privacy Policy outlines the practices of the Research Asia Directory regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our platform. This policy is generally applicable across our primary regions of operation, including Japan, Singapore, and Korea.

2. Information Collection

We do not collect sensitive personal data from our visitors. The primary purpose of the Research Asia Directory is to provide professional, business-to-business (B2B) information about market research agencies.

The information displayed on this platform (such as agency names, locations, contact emails, and service descriptions) is strictly limited to data that is either:

  • Publicly available on the respective agency's official website or public registries.
  • Voluntarily submitted to us directly by the agency representatives with a request to be listed on our directory.

3. Modification Rights

Any research agency listed on our platform holds the right to request the modification or complete removal of their public profile. To do so, please use the "Add / Update Your Agency" form on the main page, or contact us directly.

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at: directory@research-asia.com
